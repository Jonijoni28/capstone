National Service Training Program

The NSTP was established by virtue of RA 9163 also known as the National Service Training Program Act of 2001. As provided in the law, the National Service Training Program (NSTP) is a program aimed at enhancing civic consciousness and defense preparedness in the youth by developing the ethics of service and patriotism while undergoing training in any of its three (3) program components.
